Technical Refurbishment

TUWYN focuses keenly on refurbishment and redistribution (R&R) of visual systems information, information technology, and integrated technical systems. The R&R value is that the systems are in identical-to-new condition in industrial closed-loop processes, and often have the same warranties and guarantees as a brand new product.

We repair and refurbish products that have been tested and verified to function properly, and we redistribute them to the public domain in proper working order, free from defects.

  • Items used in field tests, sales displays, or demonstrations
  • Items returned for reasons other than defect, and tested by the manufacturer
  • Items returned to the manufacturer because the box or item was damaged via shipping
  • Leftover equipment sold by a downsized company to a third-party refurbisher
  • Previously-leased units that are returned and resold after the lease ends
  • Used electronics that have been returned to an electronic recycling program.
