About Us

TUWYN LLC is a certified Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB), and Small Women and Minority (SWaM) specializing in Visual Information Management (VIM) solutions. Our operations are based in Northern Virginia and we serve public sectors across the federal, state and local governments.

Company Description

What makes TUWYN unique is our ability to use best practices to integrate commercial off-the-shelf (COTS) visual systems into an austere environment which create optimal solutions for our clients. The ability to bring together the functionality and management systems of technologies that have traditionally been separated sets us apart from the competition.

Core Values

TUWYN's philosophy is referred to as the "3 E's", Empowerment, Efficiency, and Excellence.

  • Empowerment -- Empowerment of our employees
  • Efficiency -- Efficiency in our actions
  • Excellence -- Excellence in all we do

These three core elements help us maximize client satisfaction.

Mission:

TUWYN strives to provide public-sector customers with high-quality, integrated, and comprehensive Visual Information Management solutions that are holistic and cost-effective - from start to finish.

Vision:

Managing Visual Information for our clients to make it as seamless as possible. Providing commercial sector flexibility side of public sector infrastructure.

Capabilities and Solutions

Demand for customized digital imagery integrated solutions is at an all-time high, and vendors who can deliver at reasonable prices are highly valued.

TUWYN divisions are strategically designed to ensure client satisfaction. Each unit operates independently in a centralized web--based management system. Our team of experts' disciplines range from Project Management and Lifecycle Logistics to Systems Integration.

We offer extensive experience within secured environments and augment technology solutions with highly efficient coordination, acquisition, and equipment accountability practices.

Our VIM integrated approach of Policy, Process, People, Software, and Hardware promises higher margins because highly competitive scenarios are avoided and we understand that each client solution is unique.
